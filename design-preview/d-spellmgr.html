Fireball

Prepared On sheet Pinned
Casting time
1 action
Range
150 ft (45 m)
Components
V, S, M
Duration
Instantaneous

A bright streak flashes from your pointing finger to a point you choose within range, then blossoms with a low roar into an explosion of flame. Each creature in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on that point makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking 8d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.

Flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried start burning.

At higher levels — +1d6 damage for each spell slot level above 3rd.
